Our Story

Driving the Digital
Environmental Revolution

Africa Waste Solutions was formed on 19th February 2021 in Zambia. Born from a vision to transform how we care for our environment, our company has quickly become a leader in smart waste management.

The inspiration came from Mr. Charles Zulu, who envisioned a digital platform for a Solid Waste Management monitoring network. His goal was simple yet profound: to help Zambians keep their cities clean through technology and transparency.

Today, our network addresses demands at the regional, continental, and global levels. In an era where businesses are increasingly interlinked, we provide the digital infrastructure needed to bridge the gap between waste generation and sustainable management.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our platform.

Is Africa Waste Solutions ZEMA certified?

Yes, our platform is fully certified and recognized by the Zambia Environmental Management Agency (ZEMA) for waste tracking and environmental compliance monitoring.

How do I register as a business?

Simply click the "Get Started" button, select the "Business" or "Manufacturer" account type, and follow our digital KYC (Know Your Customer) process.

Does it cost anything for households?

Registration for households is free. You only pay for the waste collection services provided by our verified local partners at competitive city rates.
