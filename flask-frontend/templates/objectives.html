Self-Waste Separation

Implementing effective separation techniques at the point of generation. We empower households and businesses to sort waste at the source, ensuring high-quality recyclables and reducing contamination.

View Separation Guide

Data-Based EPR

Leveraging data for Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR). We track product lifecycles to ensure manufacturers are accountable for the end-of-life management of their products.

Transparency Accountability

Handler Safety

Prioritizing safety for waste handlers through specialized provisions and protective gear. We mandate strict safety protocols and provide necessary equipment for all waste attendants.

Safety Protocols

Clean Raw Materials

Maintaining clean raw materials for reuse and recycling. We ensure that recovered materials are processed and stored correctly to maintain their quality for the circular economy.

Circular Economy

Guided Budgeting

Guided proportional budget on solid waste generation, collection, and recycling. We advocate for data-driven financial planning to allocate resources effectively across the waste management value chain.

Financial Planning
